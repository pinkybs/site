What is visual quality?

Visual quality, like vision, definition, comfort and other indicators, has more attributes than visual acuity alone. Two patients achieve corrected visual acuity of 1.0 but may have quite different visual quality. Although they can distinguish the open direction of the letter "E", one can see its clear demarcation and the other sees it vaguely, indicating that the visual quality of the latter patient is not good. Decreased visual quality can be manifested as starburst, glare, halo, ghost and many other symptoms.

Although the visual acuity (1.0) is same, the visual quality is quite different.
